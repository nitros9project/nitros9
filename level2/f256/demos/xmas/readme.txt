F256 Christmas Demo by Roger Taylor

The pictures are in Windows Bitmap format, 320x240, 8 bits per pixel,
256 colors, dithered. Dithering yields more perceived colors and less
color bands.

How did I make the picture files?  I made some from Paint.Net and some
using the older LView graphics program, two favorites of mine. The pics
were basically copied and pasted from misc places on the web.

The music files are in MUSICA II format.  The instrument waveforms in
each .mus file are ignored and only the 4 frequencies are mixed into
their respective tones of the SN76489 chip in the F256.

xmas.run contains an alternating sequence of View and Musica commands
where an image is shown then its respective song is played.

If you plan to experiment with your own pictures and music files, please
create another *.run script so everyone can experience the different
demo flavors.
